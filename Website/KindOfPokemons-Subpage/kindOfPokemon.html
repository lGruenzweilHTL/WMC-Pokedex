Different types of Pokémon

General Pokémons

These are Pokémon’s, who can be easily catch in the wildness. Depends on the region, some Pokémon’s are commoner than others and there are many from the same kind.

Starter Pokémons

Very seldom in the wildness. When you start your journey, you can pick up from three Pokémons only ones. You can decide between a fire, water or grass type Pokémon. In the main games, you won’t get the other starter through the main story. In the series, there were some moments where starter Pokémons where in the wildness, but these are only exceptions. Starters have always a 3-state evolution line.

Legendary Pokémons

There are many legendary Pokémon’s in every region, but there is only one of his kind. These are powerful Pokémon’s and in every main game and always the cover Pokémon for the game. You can always catch this Pokémon through the story and have it in your team.

Mythical Pokémons

They like legendary Pokémon’s. There is only one of his kind and have a high base stat total. The only difference is, they’re much harder to get. You can have a legendary throw the story, while mythical are only to get on special events, where you can have them only once.

Pseudo Legendary Pokémons

These are Pokémon’s, who are basically general Pokémon’s, but way stronger than an average or good Pokémon. Every one of them has a 3-state evolution line and always a base stat total of 600. To compare it, a high base stat total for general Pokémon are 500 and legendary have over 700, so they are in the perfect middle.

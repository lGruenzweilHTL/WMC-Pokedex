Clefable

Clefable is a calm and gentle Pokémon that loves to dance under the full moon. Its sensitive ears can pick up distant sounds, making it highly aware of its surroundings. Shy and elusive, it is rarely seen in the wild but becomes a loyal friend to those who earn its trust.

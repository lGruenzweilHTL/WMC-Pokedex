Venusaur

The final evolution of Bulbasaur, Venusaur is a massive Pokémon with a fully bloomed flower on its back. This flower releases a soothing fragrance to calm others. Venusaur is deeply connected to nature and thrives in sunny weather, using its flower to harness sunlight for powerful moves.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a small, amphibian-like Pokémon with a rounded body covered in blue-green skin speckled with darker patches. A large, green plant bulb grows on its back, which stores nutrients and energy. Its wide, curious eyes and stubby limbs give it a friendly and approachable appearance. The bulb is said to grow as Bulbasaur matures, hinting at its symbiosis with the plant.

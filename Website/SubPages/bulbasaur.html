Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a small Pokémon with blue-green skin speckled with darker patches. It has curious eyes and stubby limbs, making it friendly in appearance. The green plant bulb on its back stores energy and grows as Bulbasaur matures, reflecting its bond with the plant.

Zubat

Zubat is a small, bat-like Pokémon that thrives in dark caves, rarely venturing out into the sunlight. It uses ultrasonic waves to navigate its environment and communicate with others of its kind. Though it has no eyes, Zubat is highly skilled at sensing obstacles and predators in complete darkness.

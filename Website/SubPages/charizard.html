Charizard

Charizard evolves from Charmeleon and gains the ability to fly. It’s a majestic yet intimidating Pokémon that breathes intense flames, capable of melting almost anything. Charizard has a fiery spirit and loves soaring high above the clouds, searching for worthy foes to battle.

Pokemon Emerald

  • Cover-Pokemon: Rayquaza
  • Release: 16. September 2004
  • Region: Hoenn
  • Plattform: Game Boy Advance
  • New Pokemons: 135

Villain Team

Team Aqua serves as the primary villainous group in Pokémon Sapphire, led by Archie and obsessed with expanding the seas of the Hoenn region. Their plan hinges on awakening Kyogre, a legendary Pokémon capable of causing massive rainfall and flooding the land. Team Aqua’s members appear throughout Hoenn, often disrupting local life and challenging anyone who stands in their path, all in pursuit of their vision for a world of boundless ocean.

Team Magma is the main villainous group in Pokémon Ruby, driven by their leader Maxie’s plan to expand the landmasses of the Hoenn region. They believe more land will benefit humans and Pokémon alike, so they seek to awaken the legendary Pokémon Groudon, whose power can intensify sunlight and dry up oceans. Members of Team Magma appear throughout the region, disrupting normal life and challenging anyone who stands in their way, all in the name of their single-minded goal to create a world of endless land.

Story and Features

Pokémon Emerald takes place in the Hoenn region, where both Team Magma and Team Aqua are causing trouble by trying to awaken powerful legendary Pokémon. You begin as a young Trainer who collects Gym Badges and battles tough opponents, hoping to become the Champion. During your journey, you will meet Groudon, Kyogre, and Rayquaza, each with its own incredible abilities. The game includes new features like the Battle Frontier, where you can test your skills in different arenas and earn special rewards. Enhanced animations and some storyline changes make Pokémon Emerald a fresh and exciting adventure on the Game Boy Advance.

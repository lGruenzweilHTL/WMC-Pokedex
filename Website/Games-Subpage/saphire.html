Pokemon Saphire

  • Cover-Pokemon: Kyogre
  • Release: 21. November 2002
  • Region: Hoen
  • Plattform: Game Boy Advance
  • New Pokemons: 135

Villain Team

Team Aqua serves as the primary villainous group in Pokémon Sapphire, led by Archie and obsessed with expanding the seas of the Hoenn region. Their plan hinges on awakening Kyogre, a legendary Pokémon capable of causing massive rainfall and flooding the land. Team Aqua’s members appear throughout Hoenn, often disrupting local life and challenging anyone who stands in their path, all in pursuit of their vision for a world of boundless ocean.

Story and Features

Pokémon Sapphire takes place in the Hoenn region, a vast land filled with new Pokémon to collect and train. You begin as a young Pokémon Trainer who gathers Gym Badges, aims for the Champion title, and works to stop Team Aqua from carrying out its mission to awaken Kyogre, a legendary Pokémon that can expand the ocean. As you trek through forests, sail across seas, and visit hidden places, you’ll build a strong team to overcome tough battles and discover secrets. This game includes double battles, allowing you to fight using two Pokémon at once, and features Pokémon Contests where you show off each Pokémon’s unique moves and style. You can also create a Secret Base to decorate and share. With its lively graphics and advanced gameplay on the Game Boy Advance, Pokémon Sapphire offers a fun and memorable experience that many fans still cherish.

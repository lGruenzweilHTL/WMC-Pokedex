Pokemon Ruby

  • Cover-Pokemon: Groundon
  • Release: 21. November 2002
  • Region: Hoenn
  • Plattform: Game Boy Advance
  • New Pokemons: 135

Villain Team

Team Magma is the main villainous group in Pokémon Ruby, driven by their leader Maxie’s plan to expand the landmasses of the Hoenn region. They believe more land will benefit humans and Pokémon alike, so they seek to awaken the legendary Pokémon Groudon, whose power can intensify sunlight and dry up oceans. Members of Team Magma appear throughout the region, disrupting normal life and challenging anyone who stands in their way, all in the name of their single-minded goal to create a world of endless land.

Story and Features

Pokémon Ruby is set in the Hoenn region, a place filled with new Pokémon to discover and train. You play as a young Pokémon Trainer who sets out from a small town to collect Gym Badges, become the Champion, and stop a group called Team Magma from changing the world. Team Magma wants to awaken Groudon, a legendary Pokémon with the power to expand the land. As you travel through forests, deserts, and across the sea, you will face many battles and explore hidden areas. You can catch wild Pokémon, strengthen your team, and develop strategies to defeat tough opponents. The game also introduces double battles, which let you fight with two Pokémon at the same time. You can enter Pokémon Contests, where you show off your Pokémon’s special moves and style, or create a Secret Base for fun decorations and challenges. Pokémon Ruby improves on earlier titles with colorful graphics and more advanced gameplay on the Game Boy Advance, making it a memorable adventure that fans continue to enjoy.

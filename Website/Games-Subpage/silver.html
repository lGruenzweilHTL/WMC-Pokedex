Pokemon Silver

  • Cover-Pokemon: Lugia
  • Release: 6. April 2001
  • Region: Johto
  • Plattform: Game Boy Color
  • New Pokemons: 100

Villain Team

In Pokémon Gold Team Rocket appears again as the main evil group in the Johto region. They do not have their leader, Giovanni, so they try to become powerful again by cutting off Slowpoke tails and taking over the Goldenrod Radio Tower. They hope to make Giovanni return, but in the end, their plans fail. Still, their actions show how determined they are to regain their old power.

Story and Features

In Pokémon Silver, you start in the Johto region as a new Pokémon Trainer. Soon after you receive your first Pokémon, you learn that a thief stole another Pokémon from Professor Elm’s lab. This person becomes your rival, and you meet him several times during your journey. At the same time, Team Rocket, an evil group from the Kanto region, returns. They try to regain power by cutting off Slowpoke tails and taking over the Goldenrod Radio Tower. They want their missing leader, Giovanni, to come back. Your main goals in the game are to collect Pokémon, earn Gym Badges, stop Team Rocket, and become the strongest Trainer in Johto. Pokémon Silver introduces 100 new Pokémon, which brings the total number of Pokémon to 251. It also adds two new types, Dark and Steel, to make battles more interesting. The game features a day and night cycle, so some Pokémon only appear in the morning, while others show up at night. Breeding is another new feature: if you leave two compatible Pokémon at the Day Care, they can produce an Egg, which hatches into a baby Pokémon. Finally, after you win the Johto League, you can travel to the Kanto region and challenge the original Gym Leaders from Pokémon Red and Blue.

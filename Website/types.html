Types

Fire

Fire types are strong against Grass, Ice, Bug, and Steel types, but weak against Water, Rock, and Fire types. They are known for their high attack power and often have moves that can inflict burn status on opponents.

Water

Water types are strong against Fire, Ground, and Rock types, but weak against Electric and Grass types. They are versatile and often have moves that can heal themselves or their allies.

Grass

Grass types are strong against Water, Ground, and Rock types, but weak against Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug types. They are known for their ability to inflict status conditions like sleep and poison.

Electric

Electric types are strong against Water and Flying types, but weak against Ground types. They are fast and often have moves that can paralyze opponents, reducing their speed.

Ice

Ice types are strong against Grass, Ground, Flying, and Dragon types, but weak against Fire, Fighting, Rock, and Steel types. They are known for their ability to freeze opponents, rendering them unable to move.

Fighting

Fighting types are strong against Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, and Steel types, but weak against Flying, Psychic, and Fairy types. They have high physical attack power and often have moves that can break through defensive barriers.

Poison

Poison types are strong against Grass and Fairy types, but weak against Ground and Psychic types. They are known for their ability to inflict poison status, which gradually reduces the opponent's health.

Ground

Ground types are strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types, but weak against Water, Ice, and Grass types. They are immune to Electric-type moves and often have high defense and attack stats.

Flying

Flying types are strong against Grass, Fighting, and Bug types, but weak against Electric, Ice, and Rock types. They are fast and often have moves that can increase their evasion or allow them to escape from battle.

Psychic

Psychic types are strong against Fighting and Poison types, but weak against Bug, Ghost, and Dark types. They have high special attack power and often have moves that can confuse or put opponents to sleep.

Bug

Bug types are strong against Grass, Psychic, and Dark types, but weak against Fire, Flying, and Rock types. They are known for their ability to inflict status conditions and often have moves that can lower the opponent's stats.

Rock

Rock types are strong against Fire, Ice, Flying, and Bug types, but weak against Water, Grass, Fighting, Ground, and Steel types. They have high defense and often have moves that can increase their own defense or lower the opponent's speed.

Ghost

Ghost types are strong against Psychic and Ghost types, but weak against Ghost and Dark types. They are immune to Normal and Fighting-type moves and often have moves that can inflict status conditions or trap opponents in battle.

Dragon

Dragon types are strong against Dragon types, but weak against Ice, Dragon, and Fairy types. They have high stats across the board and often have powerful moves that can deal massive damage.

Dark

Dark types are strong against Psychic and Ghost types, but weak against Fighting, Bug, and Fairy types. They are immune to Psychic-type moves and often have moves that can flinch opponents or steal their held items.

Steel

Steel types are strong against Ice, Rock, and Fairy types, but weak against Fire, Fighting, and Ground types. They have high defense and resistance to many types, making them difficult to take down.

Fairy

Fairy types are strong against Fighting, Dragon, and Dark types, but weak against Poison and Steel types. They are immune to Dragon-type moves and often have moves that can heal themselves or their allies.

Normal

Normal types are not particularly strong against any type, but they are weak against Fighting types. They are immune to Ghost-type moves and often have a wide variety of moves, making them versatile in battle.

  • Strengths: Grass, Ice, Bug, Steel
  • Weaknesses: Water, Rock, Fire
  • Immunities: None

Water

  • Strengths: Fire, Ground, Rock
  • Weaknesses: Electric, Grass
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: Versatile, can heal themselves or allies

Grass

  • Strengths: Water, Ground, Rock
  • Weaknesses: Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, Bug
  • Immunities: None

Electric

  • Strengths: Water, Flying
  • Weaknesses: Ground
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: Fast, can paralyze opponents

Ice

  • Strengths: Grass, Ground, Flying, Dragon
  • Weaknesses: Fire, Fighting, Rock, Steel
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: Can freeze opponents

Fighting

  • Strengths: Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, Steel
  • Weaknesses: Flying, Psychic, Fairy
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: High physical attack power, can break through defensive barriers

Poison

  • Strengths: Grass, Fairy
  • Weaknesses: Ground, Psychic
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: Can inflict poison status

Ground

  • Strengths: Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, Steel
  • Weaknesses: Water, Ice, Grass
  • Immunities: Electric-type moves
  • Fighting Style: High defense and attack stats

Flying

  • Strengths: Grass, Fighting, Bug
  • Weaknesses: Electric, Ice, Rock
  • Immunities: Ground-type moves
  • Fighting Style: Fast, can increase evasion or escape from battle

Psychic

  • Strengths: Fighting, Poison
  • Weaknesses: Bug, Ghost, Dark
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: High special attack power, can confuse or put opponents to sleep

Bug

  • Strengths: Grass, Psychic, Dark
  • Weaknesses: Fire, Flying, Rock
  • Immunities: None

Rock

  • Strengths: Fire, Ice, Flying, Bug
  • Weaknesses: Water, Grass, Fighting, Ground, Steel
  • Immunities: None

Ghost

  • Strengths: Psychic, Ghost
  • Weaknesses: Ghost, Dark

  • Fighting Style: Can inflict status conditions or trap opponents in battle

Dragon

  • Strengths: Dragon
  • Weaknesses: Ice, Dragon, Fairy
  • Immunities: None
  • Fighting Style: High stats, powerful moves that can deal massive damage

Dark

  • Strengths: Psychic, Ghost
  • Weaknesses: Fighting, Bug, Fairy
  • Immunities: Psychic-type moves
  • Fighting Style: Can flinch opponents or steal their held items

Steel

  • Strengths: Ice, Rock, Fairy
  • Weaknesses: Fire, Fighting, Ground
  • Immunities: Poison-type moves
  • Fighting Style: High defense, resistance to many types

Fairy

  • Strengths: Fighting, Dragon, Dark
  • Weaknesses: Poison, Steel
  • Immunities: Dragon-type moves
  • Fighting Style: Can heal themselves or allies
